## Taller and shorter (Bebras 2014-SE-04)

The young beavers Anna, Britt, Charlotte, Demi and Emma — all of different heights — want to play a game with you. They line up one behind another, all facing the same direction, in an order they chose themselves. Each beaver then counts how many beavers in front of and behind her are taller.
