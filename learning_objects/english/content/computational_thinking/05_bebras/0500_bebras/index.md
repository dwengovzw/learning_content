# The Bebras Competition

The main goal of the international Bebras competition is to get participants excited about **computer science**. During the competition, participants tackle various problems that can be linked to concepts from computer science. This also tests the participants' **computational and problem-solving thinking**.

> For more information about the international Bebras competition see https://www.bebras.be/nl/home
