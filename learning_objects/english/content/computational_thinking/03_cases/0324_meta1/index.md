<decomposition>
**Decomposition**<br>
Students break down the assignment into the different phases/steps they must go through in the create-realize-evaluate process.
<ul>
    <li>Generate a design idea</li>
    <li>Establish criteria/desired actions</li>
    <li>If applicable, the teacher imposes criteria. Students can then add extra criteria or not.</li>
    <li>Design the 'robot body' (drawing)</li>
    <li>Think of 'robot actions'</li>
    <li>Necessary actuators and sensors</li>
</ul>
