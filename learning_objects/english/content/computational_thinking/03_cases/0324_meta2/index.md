<context>
**Problem Statement**<br>
Adjust the design of the social robot, taking criteria into account.
</context>
<decomposition>
**Decomposition**<br>
Students break down the assignment into the different phases/steps they must go through in the create-realize-evaluate process.
<ul>
    <li>Check the design against the criteria</li>
    <li>Adjust 'robot body and actions' to fit the criteria and constraints (e.g., available hardware)</li>
    <li>Adjust necessary actuators and sensors</li>
    <li>Update the drawing</li>
    <li>Update the algorithm</li>
</ul>
