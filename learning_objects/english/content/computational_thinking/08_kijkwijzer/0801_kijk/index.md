# The assignment/problem statement

**The assignment/problem statement is functionally relevant to the target group.**
Preferably, the assignment is linked to a final attainment target/minimum goal with content specific to the field of study. e.g. drawing up a cultivation plan fits within the application area 'biotechnics' for the subject 'technology' in the first grade B-stream.

**The assignment/problem statement addresses a socially relevant problem/issue.**

**The assignment/problem statement challenges students to use the basic concepts to arrive at a solution.**

**The assignment can lead to using a computer to solve the problem.**
