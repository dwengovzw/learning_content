# Attainment targets and educational objectives

* Science and technology

1.8 Students can articulate the function of the senses, the skeleton, and the muscles in simple terms;

2.2 Students can investigate specific functions of components in simple technical systems by handling, assembling, or disassembling;

2.3 Students can investigate why a technical system they use themselves does not function or functions poorly;

2.10 Students can determine the requirements that the technical system they want to use or create must meet;

2.18 Students can, using examples from various application areas of technology, illustrate that technical systems can be useful, dangerous, and/or harmful to themselves, to others, or to nature and the environment.


* Mathematics

1.17 Students can find estimation procedures for data that are not exactly specified or cannot be exactly determined.


* Information and communication technology

3. Students can practice independently in an ICT-supported learning environment

4. Students can learn independently in an ICT-supported learning environment

5. Students can use ICT to creatively express their own ideas.


* Learning to learn

4. Students can solve simple problems in a systematic and insightful manner.

5. Students can, possibly with guidance, monitor and adjust their own learning process.
