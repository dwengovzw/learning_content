# Borrowing Materials

The WeGoSTEM materials are offered free of charge by dwengo VZW. Keep them in good condition for the next schools.

To make the distribution of our materials go as smoothly as possible, we work together with local libraries. You can find an overview at the end of this learning object with links to the reservation form.

With the material in this box, you can give a WeGoSTEM workshop for a class of up to 30 students. Get your colleagues excited about WeGoSTEM too and give the workshop together for all students of the 5th and 6th grade!
Please return the materials to the library after two weeks!

## Contents
The box contains:
* 15 robot kits
* 1 green box with spare materials
* 1 red box with everything that is broken
* Pictures of robots to show in class
* Drawings for the activity *Program a Human*
* Example photos of how the robot can be built to hand out to each pair
