# Social graph (solution)

<div class="dwengo-content assignment">
<h2 class="title">Assignment</h2>
<div class="content">
Try to answer the following questions.
<ul>
<li>Which friend groups can you extract from the graph?<br>From strongly connected subgroups, we can assume they are friends. For example Noa, Ren and Alexi, or Robin, Eden, Sami and Yasmin.</li>
<li>Which people in this network have a lot of influence? Someone is influential when their posts can be read by many people.<br>If Ren posts something there are 5 friends and 4 friends of friends who will see the post. </li>
<li>Which people are the farthest from each other? <br> Sami and Cai, Sami and Jules, Yasmin and Cai, Yasmin and Jules.</li>
</ul>
