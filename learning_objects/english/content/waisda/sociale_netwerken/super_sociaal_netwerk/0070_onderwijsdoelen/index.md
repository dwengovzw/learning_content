# Educational goals

The lesson material in the learning path contributes to achieving the following educational goals.
These are the official wordings as published on [onderwijsdoelen.be](https://onderwijsdoelen.be/).

## 2nd stage

### Finality: further study

##### Digital competencies
* 04.02 The students purposefully use common functionalities of comparable applications to create digital content.
* 04.04 The students respect ethical, social, and legal rules when using digital technology.
* 04.05 The students analyze the impact of digital systems on society based on principles of computational thinking.
##### Mathematics - natural sciences - technology - STEM
* 06.22 The students solve questions and problems by mathematizing and demathematizing and by using heuristics.
* 06.23 The students use ICT to perform calculations and create graphical representations.
* 06.52 The students design a solution to a problem by applying science, technology, or mathematics in an integrated way.
* 06.53 The students analyze the interaction between science, technology, mathematics, and society using societal challenges.
##### Learning competencies
* 13.3 The students use school and subject-specific language.
