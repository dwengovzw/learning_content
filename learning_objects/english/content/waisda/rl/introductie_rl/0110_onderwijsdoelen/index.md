# Educational objectives

The learning materials in the learning path contribute to achieving the following educational objectives.
These are the official wordings as published on [onderwijsdoelen.be](https://onderwijsdoelen.be/).

## 2nd stage

### Transition finality

##### Digital competencies
* 04.04 Students respect ethical, social and legal rules when using digital technology.
* 04.05 Students analyze the impact of digital systems on society based on principles of computational thinking.
##### Learning competencies
* 13.1 Students reflect cyclically and in a subject-specific way on their own learning process and purposefully adjust it on that basis.
* 13.3 Students use school and subject-specific language.

### Labour market finality

##### Digital competencies
* 04.04 Students respect ethical, social and legal rules when using digital technology.
* 04.05 Students analyze the impact of digital systems on society based on principles of computational thinking.
##### Learning competencies
* 13.1 Students reflect cyclically and in a subject-specific way on their own learning process and purposefully adjust it on that basis.
* 13.3 Students use school and subject-specific language.

### Double finality

##### Digital competencies
* 04.04 Students respect ethical, social and legal rules when using digital technology.
* 04.05 Students analyze the impact of digital systems on society based on principles of computational thinking.
##### Learning competencies
* 13.1 Students reflect cyclically and in a subject-specific way on their own learning process and purposefully adjust it on that basis.
* 13.3 Students use school and subject-specific language.
* 13.4 Students purposefully search for information in various sources and process it in a critical and systematic way.
