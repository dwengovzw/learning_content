# Part 1: frequency analysis

- The participants find a small box in the room with a conversation between two historians on it.
- On the wall are various images of historians, with information about those people underneath, as well as a frequency table showing how often they use certain words.

- By compiling a frequency table for the conversation between the two historians, the participants can determine which two characters are speaking to each other. 
- The sum of their birth years is the code for the box.
