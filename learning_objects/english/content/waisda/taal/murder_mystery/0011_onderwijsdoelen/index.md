# Educational objectives

The learning materials in the learning path contribute to achieving the following educational objectives.
These are the official formulations as published on [onderwijsdoelen.be](https://onderwijsdoelen.be/).

## 2nd degree

### Progression finality

##### Digital competencies
* 04.05 Students analyze the impact of digital systems on society based on principles of computational thinking.
##### Social-relational competencies
* 05.01 Students interact with others respectfully and constructively, taking each other's boundaries into account.
##### Mathematics - natural sciences - technology - STEM
* 06.21 Students describe real-world phenomena using mathematical concepts from the second degree.
* 06.22 Students solve questions and problems by mathematizing and demathematizing and by using heuristics.
* 06.23 Students use ICT to perform calculations and create graphical representations.
* 06.52 Students design a solution to a problem by applying science, technology, or mathematics in an integrated way.
* 06.53 Students analyze the interplay between science, technology, mathematics, and society on the basis of societal challenges.
##### Learning competencies
* 13.3 Students use school and subject-specific language.
* 13.4 Students purposefully search for information in diverse sources and process it in a critical and systematic way.
##### Entrepreneurship
* 15.02 Students generate creative ideas to solve a problem and discuss their feasibility based on criteria.
