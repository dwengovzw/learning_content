# The mystery of the death of Baroness Heloisa Argentoliensis

*Baroness Heloisa Argentoliensis was found dead in the tower room of her castle. How did she die?*

In this activity, you will solve the mystery of the death of Baroness Heloisa.<br>At the same time, you will learn more about language technology and AI. 

Depending on your prior knowledge, this activity takes between two and three hours.
