# Materials

It's best to do this 'escape room' with two to four people. For larger groups, you can divide the room and provide the necessary materials for each group. 

Each group needs the following materials:
* A laptop with an internet connection. A computer mouse can also be useful.
* Two boxes that you can lock with a combination lock.
* A padlock with an adjustable 3-digit code.
* A padlock with an adjustable 4-digit code.
* A printout of the hints for unplugged activity 1 ([Characters](content/Personages.pdf), [Conversation](content/Gesprek.pdf)).
* A printout of the hints for unplugged activity 2 ([ASCII table](content/ASCII_tabel_hint.pdf)).
* A printout of the links to the Python notebooks ([Link to authorship recognition notebook](content/Notebook_auteursherkenning.pdf), [Link to cipher notebook](content/Notebook_geheimschrift.pdf)).
* A printout of the letters for authorship recognition ([Letters](content/Brieven.pdf)).
* A printout of the will ([Will](content/Testament.pdf))
* A printout of the letters in cipher ([Cipher](content/Brieven_geheimschrift.pdf)).

The documents to be printed also always indicate where they should go (e.g., *In box 1*). This way you can easily set up the activity.
