# Learning objectives

The learning materials in the learning path contribute to achieving the following educational objectives.
These are the official wordings as published on [onderwijsdoelen.be](https://onderwijsdoelen.be/).

## 2nd stage

### Finality: transition to higher education

##### Digital competencies
* 04.02 The students purposefully use common functionalities of similar applications to create digital content.
* 04.04 The students respect ethical, social, and legal rules when using digital technology.
* 04.05 The students analyse the impact of digital systems on society using principles of computational thinking.
##### Mathematics - natural sciences - technology - STEM
* 06.23 The students use ICT to perform calculations and create graphical representations.
* 06.52 The students design a solution to a problem by applying science, technology, or mathematics in an integrated way.
* 06.53 The students analyse the interaction between science, technology, mathematics, and society on the basis of societal challenges.
##### Learning competencies
* 13.1 The students reflect cyclically and subject-specifically on their own learning process and adjust it accordingly in a targeted way.
* 13.3 The students use school and subject-specific language.
