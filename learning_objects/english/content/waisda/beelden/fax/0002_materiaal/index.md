# Materials

For this activity, each group of 5 to 7 participants needs the following materials:

* Two blank grids of 20 x 16 squares ([raster.pdf](content/sjabloon_fax.pdf)).
* Two pencils.
* A space with enough room to walk back and forth within the groups.
