# Drawing emotions

You will receive an A3 sheet from your instructor with the following grid.

![](images/raster.svg)

Choose an emotion, *happy* or *surprised*, and fill the grid with smileys showing that chosen emotion. If you chose *happy*, for example, your completed grid could look like this:

![](images/voorbeeld_raster_blij.jpg)

<div class="dwengo-content important">
<h2 class="title">Attention</h2>
<div class="content">
Do not mix emotions in one grid. A sheet should contain only smileys with one specific emotion, e.g., happy!
</div>
