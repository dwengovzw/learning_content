A data-driven AI system?
===============

You probably came up with a number of rules that looked at, for example, the color, the shape, and the size of the tomatoes. Then you'd have a system that works well for "regular" tomatoes, but of course tomatoes come in all kinds of shapes and sizes. For example:

![yellow tomatoes](img/gele_tomaten.png "Yellow tomatoes")
![Different types of tomatoes](img/kleur_tomaten.png "Tomatoes in different shapes and colors")
