# Cards

![](@youtube/https://www.youtube.com/embed/XH3S4HI-BXE)

Using this card set, you can encourage students to reflect on the ethical aspects of new technologies. What about privacy? Will social contacts be compromised? Which technologies are welcomed with open arms? What is not desirable? Are the new technologies affordable for everyone?
