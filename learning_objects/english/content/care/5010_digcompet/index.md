# Digital competence and media literacy 
## Attainment targets for digital competence and media literacy 

*Attainment targets for the second stage* 
<em>
<ul><li>4.1 The students show self-confidence when exploring and using digital infrastructure and applications. (Attitudinal).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.2 The students purposefully and appropriately use standard functionalities of digital infrastructure and applications to create, share, and manage digital content. (Applying).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.3 The students purposefully and appropriately use standard functionalities of digital infrastructure and applications to communicate, collaborate, and participate in initiatives digitally. (Applying).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.4 The students explain how the building blocks of digital systems relate to and interact with each other. (Understanding).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.5 (Progression finality) The students design algorithms to solve problems digitally. (Creating).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.6 (Progression and double finality) and 4.5 (Labor-market finality) The students explain mutual influences between, on the one hand, the individual and, on the other hand, media, digital infrastructure, and digital applications. (Understanding).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.7 (Progression and double finality) and 4.6 (Labor-market finality) The students apply the rules of the digital world. (Applying).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.8 (Progression and double finality) and 4.7 (Labor-market finality) The students evaluate the possibilities and risks of their own media behavior and that of others. (Evaluating).</li></ul> 
</em>

*Attainment targets for the third stage* 
<em>
<ul><li>4.1 The students show self-confidence when exploring and using digital infrastructure and applications. (Attitudinal).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.2 The students purposefully and appropriately use functionalities of digital infrastructure and applications to create, share, and manage digital content. (Applying).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.3 The students purposefully and appropriately use functionalities of digital infrastructure and applications to communicate, collaborate, and participate in initiatives digitally. (Applying).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.4 The students evaluate the building blocks of digital systems with regard to their own use and their use in a societal context. (Evaluating).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.5 (Progression finality) The students program solutions for problems according to a certain methodology by means of self-designed algorithms. (Creating).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.6 (Progression and double finality) and 4.5 (Labor-market finality) The students consistently demonstrate appropriate media behavior. (Attitudinal).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.7 (Progression and double finality) and 4.6 (Labor-market finality) The students critically assess mutual influences between, on the one hand, the individual and society and, on the other hand, media, digital infrastructure, and digital applications. (Evaluating).</li></ul> 
<ul><li>4.8 (Progression and double finality) and 4.7 (Labor-market finality) The students critically apply the rules of the digital world. (Applying).</li></ul>
</em>
