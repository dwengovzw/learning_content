# DwenguinoBlockly
## Introduction - teacher

DwenguinoBlockly is a programming environment designed for children in the upper years of primary education and the first years of secondary education. The environment uses a graphical programming language and gives students the opportunity to program a robot. 

Within the programming environment, a simulator is provided to test programs. In the simulator you can choose from different scenarios, such as a driving robot, a social robot, and a spirograph (drawing robot). 
For those who do not have physical materials available, DwenguinoBlockly can therefore also be used autonomously as a teaching tool. A physical robot is not necessary.

For those who do have physical materials available, the simulator can be used to test a program before trying it out on the physical robot. If something then doesnâ€™t work, you avoid the dilemma of not knowing whether the problem is in the program or with the physical robot.
